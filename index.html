Yogyakarta, Indonesia
11–12 July 2017

Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of the organizing committee, we are pleased to announce that the 3rd International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST 2017) will be held on 11–12 July 2017 in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. This ICST 2017 is part of the series of Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conferences (UGM-ASC 2017). It provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies, and directions in Engineering and Natural Sciences issues.

The ICST 2017 consists of four symposia including the 5th ANNUM Conference, the 2nd Computer symposium, the 2nd Geomaritime symposia and the 1st OMICS: from genomics to metabolomics (see in the satellite events). The accepted full papers will be published in journals that are indexed by Scopus or DOAJ / EBSCO i.e. Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, Indonesian Journal of Geography, Indonesian journal of Biotechnology, and IEEE Xplore digital library.
