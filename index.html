#ICST2017

Held as a part of Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conferences (UGM-ASC 2017) series, The 3rd International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST 2017) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies, and directions in engineering and natural sciences issues.

This year, four symposia will be held, consisting of the 5th ANNUM Conference, the 2nd Computer symposium, the 2nd Geomaritime symposium and the 1st OMICS Symposium: from Genomics to Metabolomics.

The accepted full papers will be published in journals that are indexed by Scopus or DOAJ/EBSCO: the Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, Indonesian Journal of Geography, Indonesian Journal of Biotechnology, or IEEE Xplore digital library.
