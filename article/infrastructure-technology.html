Guidelines for full paper submission

Topics

Probability theory, stochastic processes, and statistics; numerical approximation and analysis; computational techniques and simulations; general theory in fluid dynamics; computational methods in fluid dynamics; laminar flows; flow instabilities; turbulent flows; vortex dynamics; rotating fluids; hydrodynamic waves; flows through porous media; suspensions; diffusion and aggregation; sedimentation and migration; granular flow; applied fluid mechanics; static elasticity; static buckling and instability; viscoelasticity, plasticity, viscoplasticity; vibrations and mechanical waves; fracture mechanics, fatigue and cracks; materials science; industrial and technological research and development; transportation (land, water and air transportation); environmental safety; environmental regulations; impact of natural and man-made disasters; environmental aspects; municipal and industrial waste; water quality; climate and inter-annual variability; urban planning and construction; tsunamis; earthquake; volcano seismology; marine geology and geophysics (ocean drilling, submarine landslides); ocean waves and oscillations; turbulence, diffusion, and mixing processes in oceanography; coastal, estuarine, and near shore processes; sediment transport; hydrography; sedimentation; modeling; general theory; drought; erosion and sedimentation; debris flow and landslides; evapotranspiration; ground water; soil moisture; eco-hydrology; water resources; hydrologic cycles and budgets; irrigation and dams; wetlands; hydrometeorology and hydroclimatology.

Publication & submission details

Selected papers will be published in proceedings indexed by Scopus.
