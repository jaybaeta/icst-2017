Thank you.

The 3rd International Conference on Science and Technology saw scientists and professionals congregate for two days of discussion and debate in the middle of a dry July. Researchers from fields as disparate as chemistry and maritime geopolitics came together to share their latest findings, in a display of the interdisciplinary collaborative spirit that will embody the future of technological advancement. Much was learned, and even more was revealed in the way of where the progress of science and technology will go next.

To all the participants and speakers who joined us, thank you for making the ICST 2017 an event to remember.

See you next year.

— The ICST  2017 Committee
