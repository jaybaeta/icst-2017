5th ANNUM Symposium

Topics

Organic chemistry and life sciences, biomaterials, organic polymers, supramolecular chemistry, nanomaterials and nanotechnology, analytical, environmental and green chemistry, physical and theoretical chemistry, inorganic chemistry, catalysis, applied chemistry, chemical engineering, material science and technology.

Publication

Indonesian Journal of Chemistry (IJC); indexed by Scopus.
